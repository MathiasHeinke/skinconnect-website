## Business Model Comparison

### Revenue Streams

#### Direct-to-Consumer (Curology, Hims)
- **Subscription Revenue**: 70-85% des Umsatzes
- **Average Order Value**: 25-40 USD
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: 300-500 USD
- **Churn Rate**: 13-20% jährlich

#### B2B Licensing (ModiFace, Proven)
- **License Fees**: 100K-1M USD upfront
- **Usage-based Revenue**: 0.10-0.50 USD per transaction
- **Royalty Rates**: 2-8% auf Gross Sales
- **Partnership Duration**: 3-5 Jahre Verträge

#### Omnichannel Integration (Sephora)
- **Retail Revenue**: 65% stationärer Verkauf
- **Digital Revenue**: 35% E-commerce + Apps
- **Service Revenue**: Beauty Services (8% des Umsatzes)
- **Partner Revenue**: Brand Licensing (3% des Umsatzes)

### Key Performance Indicators

#### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: 25-150 USD (je nach Channel)
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: 150-500 USD
- **Monthly Churn Rate**: 2-8%
- **Net Promoter Score**: 60-80 (above industry average)

#### Financial Metrics
- **Gross Margin**: 70-85% (Subscription), 45-65% (Retail)
- **Operating Margin**: 15-25% (mature businesses)
- **Revenue Growth**: 25-150% YoY (scale-up phase)
- **Customer Payback Period**: 6-18 Monate

#### Operational Metrics
- **Time to Market**: 12-24 Monate für neue Features
- **Customer Support**: <24h response time
- **Platform Uptime**: 99.9%+ Verfügbarkeit
- **Data Processing**: <3 Sekunden für AI-Analyse
